COMPANY PURCHASING POLICY
Version 2.0 - Effective January 2024

APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS:
1. All purchases over $5,000 require pre-approval from department manager
2. Purchases over $10,000 require CFO approval
3. Purchases over $50,000 require Board approval

VENDOR REQUIREMENTS:
4. IT equipment must be purchased from approved vendors only
5. New vendors must complete vendor onboarding process before first purchase
6. All vendors must have current W-9 on file

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS:
7. Travel expenses must include trip justification and itemized receipts
8. Consulting fees over $10,000 require a Statement of Work (SOW)
9. All equipment purchases require asset tagging within 5 business days

CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RULES:
10. Software licenses require IT department approval
11. Marketing expenses require Marketing VP approval
12. Training expenses require HR approval for amounts over $2,000

PAYMENT TERMS:
13. Standard payment terms are Net 30
14. Early payment discounts must be approved by Finance
15. Credit card purchases limited to $1,000 per transaction

COMPLIANCE:
16. All purchases must comply with company code of conduct
17. Personal purchases are strictly prohibited
18. Gifts from vendors exceeding $50 must be reported to Compliance
